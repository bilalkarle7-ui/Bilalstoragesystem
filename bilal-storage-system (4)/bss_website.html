About Us

Pioneering Storage Solutions since 1994

Bilal Storage System, established in 1994, is a trusted manufacturer and supplier of high-quality storage solutions based in Mumbai. With over three decades of experience, we specialize in designing durable and customizable racks.

Customer satisfaction is at our core. We focus on delivering reliable, cost-effective, and tailored storage systems for industrial and warehouse applications.
